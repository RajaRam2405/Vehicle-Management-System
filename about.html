The Vehicle Management System is a smart solution developed specifically for vehicle service shops to simplify daily operations and improve customer service. It allows shop administrators to register vehicles, schedule and assign service tasks to mechanics, and keep track of ongoing and completed work. Mechanics can easily access their assigned jobs, update service statuses, and ensure smooth workflow from start to finish. Built using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, and MySQL on the XAMPP platform, the system helps reduce paperwork, avoid scheduling conflicts, and maintain detailed service history for each vehicle. This ensures faster service, better organization, and increased customer satisfaction.

With an intuitive dashboard and real-time task updates, the system brings transparency and efficiency to workshop management. Service records are stored securely and can be accessed anytime, helping shop owners make informed decisions. The platform is designed to be user-friendly, even for those with minimal technical skills. Whether handling routine check-ups or emergency repairs, our system supports better planning and smoother service delivery. It's the perfect tool to take your service shop operations to the next level.
